# TestimonialFlow

**Collect testimonials. Embed anywhere. In 2 minutes.**

TestimonialFlow is the simplest way to collect customer testimonials and display them on your website with a beautiful embed widget.

## How It Works

1. **Create a form** — Set up a testimonial collection form in seconds
2. **Share with customers** — Send them a link. They leave a review (name, role, text, star rating)
3. **Embed anywhere** — Copy one line of code. Testimonials appear on your site instantly

## Features

- Collection forms with shareable links
- Dashboard to manage, approve, and curate testimonials
- Embeddable widget (single line of JS)
- Free tier: 10 testimonials, 1 form
- Pro: $9/mo for 100 testimonials, unlimited forms, no branding
